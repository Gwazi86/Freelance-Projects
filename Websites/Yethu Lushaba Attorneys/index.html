About

Yethu Lushaba Inc

Yethu Lushaba Inc is a 100% black and female owned law firm situated in Nelspruit, Mpumalanga.

The Practice was established on 01 June 2017 by its founding director Ms. Yethu Lushaba who established the firm to provide quality legal services to the public.

We are a growing small practice intending to compete with the "considered" leading firms in the province. Our firm is not defined by its size but rather by the quality of its work.

The Yethu Lushaba Inc team is a family of young, dedicated, passionate and hardworking individuals who strive to offer honest and professional services by simply upholding values of integrity, paying attention to detail and solving legal issues.

About Yethu Lushaba

Yethu Lushaba-Lekhuleni obtained her law degree from the University of Witwatersrand. She is passionate about property and commercial law. She was admitted as an Attorney in May 2017 and started practicing as such as of 1 June 2017.

Yethu Lushaba-Lekhuleni serves as a Council member of the Mpumalanga Legal Practice Council and is the deputy chairperson of the National Association of Democratic Lawyers Mbombela. Yethu is in the forefront for positive transformation in the Legal Fraternity.

Yethu’s hobbies include reading, travelling, interior decorating, cooking and spending time with her family.

Commercial Law

• We provide our clients with a wide variety of commercial law services, each one structured for a specific client.


• We draft and assess commercial contracts, register and administer business trust and provide advice on the most efficient way to structure agreements, deals and other day to day business requirements.


• We assist clients with labour law related matters, hold disciplinary hearings on behalf of employers, advice employers on procedurally correct labour practices and/or represent employees by assisting them prepare for a disciplinary hearing and CCMA appearances. We further draft and administer employment agreements and company policies.

Real Estate And Property Law

• We offer advice and provide strategy in the field of real property by assisting investors and buyers to take appropriate decisions regarding property purchases.


• We further advise and provide strategies in structuring transactions aimed at enabling previously disadvantaged individuals or groups to acquire ownership in commercial or agricultural property ventures.


• Assist clients in making land claims and/or negotiate settlements on land claim actions.


• We specialise in all aspects of property law such as agricultural, commercial and residential property transactions including evictions.


• We offer assistance in the transfer of properties.


• We further advice on cluster and sectional title developments, sale and purchase agreements, options, leases, development agreements and building contracts.

Estate Planning

• We specialise in providing individuals, families and business owners with comprehensive personalised estate planning services encompassing; advising and drafting wills. Advising, registering and administrating trusts and further winding up the estate after death.


• We are able to offer expertise in estate planning, tax planning, preservation of assets after death, protecting assets against spendthrift children etc.

Marriage Law

• We advise on the different matrimonial property systems.

• We draft and register Anti-Nuptial contracts.

• We either commence or defend Divorce actions.

Litigation

We commence or either defend actions/applications on behalf of Clients for matters in the Magistrate Court, High Court and the Labour Court relating to:


• Commercial litigation

• Property related disputes

• Claims on damages

• Minister of Police
