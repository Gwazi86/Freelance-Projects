About Us

An amazing food experience, Kwami Catering specialises in ethnic and Modern South African cuisine.

Kwami Catering is a proudly South African, 100 percent black female owned and run full catering service company based in Midrand Gauteng. It was founded in 2015 by Bathabile Sambo, an avid cook with a curious palate.

The combination of her passion for food and experiences in food service led to establishment of this entity. We're known for our unique food flavours, exceptional service and great value for money.

MANAGEMENT AND OPERATIONAL STAFF
KWAMI CATERING STAFF

Bathabile Sambo, the owner and operator of Kwami Catering, has combined her resources and skills gained from her previous experience of supporting her mother's catering company for several years. She has gained valuable experience as a cook under her mother's guidance. With her background in sales, Bathabile brings a unique energy and enthusiasm to the company, and is responsible for marketing and business development, ensuring the relevance of the business. She is also hands-on in the kitchen, ensuring efficient service delivery during events.
